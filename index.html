About Us

The Department of Textile Materials Engineering at BUTEX is dedicated to advancing education, research, and industry engagement in the textile sector. We nurture skilled professionals ready to take on the challenges of a rapidly evolving global industry.

Quality Education

We offer a curriculum combining theory, practice, and innovation for holistic learning.

Research & Innovation

Promoting cutting-edge research to address real-world textile challenges.

Industry Link

Strong collaboration with national and international textile industries.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

  • Recognized as a global leader in textile education.
  • Pioneering sustainable textile innovations.
  • Fostering global research partnerships.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality, industry-relevant education.
  • Advance textile research and innovation.
  • Develop leaders for the textile sector.

Objectives

Encourage sustainable textile production.
